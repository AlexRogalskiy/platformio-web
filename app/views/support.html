Frequently Asked Questions

Which products are covered by the Developer and Enterprise plans?
The premium support plans apply to all PlatformIO.Org and PIO Plus products: PlatformIO Core, PlatformIO IDE, PIO Unified Debugger, PIO Unit Testing, PIO Remote, Integration with Cloud and Desktop IDEs.

Please note that premium support does not apply to community integration in PlatformIO's Development Platforms and 3rd party software (framework, SDK, etc.).

Can I purchase premium support only when I need it?

Yes. The Developer premium support plan may be purchased on a monthly basis as needed.

How does the web conference support work?
Enterprise customers can request a web conference call with our support engineer when submitting a support ticket. After the ticket is placed, a support engineer will review your issue, and email you dial-in information within the target SLA of your support plan.
